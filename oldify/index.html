Welcome to Oldify!

Have you run out of money to buy a vinyl record? Did your record player break? Do you want to hear that Queen song but don't have that record? Welcome to Oldify, the website where, for just a few dollars, you have access to all the songs.

This website is still in beta, but we believe that in a few decades we will be ready to move forward. Meanwhile, access is free. More songs comming soon!
